VINDALOO CURRY PASTE

Our Vindaloo Curry Paste is a unique concentrated blend of spices, rich in texture and bursting with flavour. Reminiscent of Southern India, the aroma and taste will heighten your culinary experience.

Instructions: This is a concentrated paste and we therefore recommend only 1 tbsp per two servings with the protein of your choice: legumes, fish, tofu, pork etc. Add extra paste for a stronger flavour. 

Ingredients : Spices, sunflower oil, cider vinegar, onions, distilled white vinegar, sugar, salt,ginger and garlic.
Allergens: Contains mustard seed.


Try our delicious Dahl recipe using this paste!
